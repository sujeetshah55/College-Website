About JNTUK

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be a globally recognized center of academic excellence, fostering innovation, and driving societal change.

Our mission is to provide rigorous, inclusive education and conduct cutting-edge research that addresses the world's most pressing challenges. We are committed to developing critical thinkers and ethical leaders.

Our History

JNTUK, tracing its origins to 1946, has evolved from a technical college to a major state university dedicated to technological advancement and educational excellence.

Our dedication to practical learning and academic rigor has remained constant, cementing our reputation as a leader in engineering and science education across Andhra Pradesh.
